A closer look at J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone was made in 2001 and is the first of 8 in the movie franchise based on J.K. Rowling's books. It tells the story of a boy wizard named Harry Potter who was destined to defeat the great evil wizard who terrorized both the Magical and Normal worlds. In this first installation, Harry enters the Magic world for the first time and attends the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns the truth about his parents, and encounters evil in the most unexpected manner.

Characters

  • Harry Potter, the main character
  • Ron Weasley, red-headed and loyal friend
  • Hermoine Granger, adorable and brilliant friend of Harry's, also from the muggle world
  • Album Dumbledore, the head of school
  • Hagrid, the gate keeper
