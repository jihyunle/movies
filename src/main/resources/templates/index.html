Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

A closer look at J.K. Rowling's beloved character


The film Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone came out in 2001 and is the first of eight in the movie franchise based on J.K. Rowling's books. It tells the story of a boy wizard named Harry Potter who was destined to defeat the great evil wizard Voldemort, who terrorized both the Wizard and Human worlds.

Throughout the series, the viewers see Harry grow into his role as the "chosen one", as he manages to rise to the occasions in times of trouble, with the support of his friends and family. Beloved by children, young adults and adults all alike, Harry Potter has been an integral part of the 90's kids life, and has grown into a francise over the decade long period. It continues to bewitch new audiences of this generation, and can be experienced in books, movies, and theatre adaptations, as well as in 4D theme park.


In this first installation, Harry enters the Magic world for the first time and attends the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in England, where he learns the truth about his parents, and encounters evil in the most unexpected manner.
